About Us

Rizq Welfare Organization is a non-profit initiative dedicated to ending hunger and ensuring no one in our community sleeps hungry. We believe in the power of sharing, compassion, and collective action.

Our Mission

To bring hope to empty plates by delivering nutritious meals to the underprivileged, with compassion, dignity, and respect.

Our Vision

A world where no one sleeps hungry and everyone shares their Rizq with those in need.
